About Me

Hello! I'm Mark Wylde, a web developer passionate about building simple, efficient solutions. I believe in the philosophy of "You Ain't Gonna Need It" (YAGNI) and enjoy working on projects that solve real problems without unnecessary complexity.

My journey into web development began with a love for creating things that work well and are easy to understand. Since then, I've honed my skills in JavaScript, Node.js, and various web technologies. I'm always eager to learn new things and explore innovative solutions.

My Approach

I believe great software comes from understanding both the technical challenges and the human needs behind them. Whether I'm writing code, reviewing architecture, or working with a team, I always ask: "How can we make this simpler and better?"

What I Focus On

  • Building web applications with modern JavaScript
  • Creating simple, maintainable backend systems
  • Open source contributions and community building
  • Sharing knowledge through writing and speaking
